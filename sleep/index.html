There are a couple of rules though when it comes to making your cat sleep like a baby.
  • The ground should be as hard as it possibly can, lay your tiger on a floor. Tiles preferably.
  • The cat must be surrounded by some kind of non-transparent matter. Plastic will fit. Or fabric. Or whatever he decides.
  • The size of the bedroom should allow the cat to get into it and pretty much nothing else.
